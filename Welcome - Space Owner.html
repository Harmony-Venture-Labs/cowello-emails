Share Your Space

We give you unlimited locations. Each location can include multiple rooms. You can attach specific equipment to a room or make it available for reservation by adding it to the location’s inventory. Create your location(s), add your rooms, and add available inventory to enable your space for online booking requests.

Invite Your Community

Preview what your members will see by inviting a ‘test’ member at a secondary personal email address. Then invite real members of your health and wellness coworking community to CoWello! After you send an invite, you will be able to track the status and view whether the invitation has been accepted or is still pending.
